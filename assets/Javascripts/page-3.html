My photography experience:

I have 9 years of experience photographing babies and families, and after having my own children I became more interested in photoshop, and making composites.

It became a passion of mine, and it's what lead me to start working with digital retouching.

My goal now is learning more about studio photography and manipulating light. It all influences in a good composition and retouching techniques.
