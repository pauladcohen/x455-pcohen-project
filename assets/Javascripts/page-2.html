Who am I?

Hello There!

My Name is Paula Donatelli Cohen, and I'm a Brazilian girl living in the Bay Area, CA.
I've been in the USA for 12 years, and I am now naturalized an American citizen.

I hold a BA in Media Communication, I possess a certificate in Entertainment Media from UCLA Extension, and I'm currently taking the 'Professional Graphic Design' certificate program at UCBerkley Extension.

I have 14 years experience working within many areas of Digital Communication, of which 9 years were in Photography, with the past couple years dedicated to Image Retouching. Working with photoshop has become a passion of mine, and being able to make images look better and be creative doing so, has made it even more attractive to me.

One of my favorite hobbies is taking photos of my children and creating composites to make it look magical and different.
My family is my favorite people in the world, and I love spending time with them; but lets be honest, having a little me time, even if it is working outside the house, feels really good and it's actually really important to ones mental health and relationships.
This is why my work is so important to me and why I value the working space so much.
