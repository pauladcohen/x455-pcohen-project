Graphic Design Projects

Projects made for 'Professional Graphic Design' certificate program from UCBerkley Extension.

Posters

The first poster was made for my Photoshop class. We needed to create a new poster from our favorite movie.

I chose the classic movie 'The Magic Of OZ', because it was a movie that marked my childhood.

I tried to add to the image the most important characters and place; unfortunatelly I didn't find a quality image from the wicked witch, which is my favorite character in the movie


The second poster was made for the same class, but the idea was to make an AD Campain for a product using some famous art

Again, I chose a Brazilian product called 'Havaianas'; this brand is famous worldwide for its sandals, but also sell hats and purses for the beach.

I chose some famous arts also from Brazilians Artists for the Campain.

Magazine Cover

The first magazine was made for my InDesign class. We needed to create a complete magazine with Cover and all the content and articles inside.

I chose to do a Turism related magazine with the intent of showing people a place in Brazil that is not very popular, but to me, it's a hidden Gem.

The place is called Bonito, and it means beautiful in Portugues.

The cover is an image of it's water cave, and a mermaid is jumping in it. It was an image taken by a professional photographer to display in some kind of AD.


The second Magazine Cover was made for my Photoshop class; and you may recognize the image from my portfolio, because I used my own image for this cover.

This image has a special meaning to me, because my son was born at the first month of the pandemic, and it was the thoughest moment of my life.

Based on my motherhood experience, I chose the articles that are going in the cover, as I feel other women also struggle with the same thing.
